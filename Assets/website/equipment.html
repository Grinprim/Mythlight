Equipment

Weapons, armor, and gear define how you survive Mythlight’s world.

Damage Types

Puncture Precise, piercing strikes that penetrate defenses.
Slash Cutting attacks that cause bleeding wounds.
Blunt Heavy, armor-crushing force and impact.
Cleave Wide sweeping blows that hit multiple targets.
Blast Explosive area of effect impact.

Equipment Tags

Concealable

Small enough to be hidden beneath normal clothing without detection.

Flexible

Does not impose penalties on stealth or acrobatic movements.

Fireproof

Reduces incoming fire damage by 1.

Ballistic

Reduces incoming firearm/projectile damage by 1.

Restrictive

–1 Reflex. Disables Evasion bonuses.

Two-Handed

Requires two hands to wield effectively.

Compact

Can be used in the off-hand.

Improvised

Breaks permanently after 3 successful hits.

Powered

Negates 1 point of Restrictive penalty.

Brewing & Alchemy

Brewing combines ingredients with defined aspects and tiers to produce potions, poisons, and volatile mixtures. Outcomes depend on balance, precision, and ingredient synergy.

Brewing Rules
  • Select ingredients totaling at least one full Aspect Bitcode.
  • Majority ingredient tier determines Potion Tier.
  • Disparity between aspects increases difficulty.
  • Failure may weaken, alter, or destabilize the brew.
