System Overview

Master the mechanics of a world built on simple rolls and epic consequences.

2d6 Core Engine

Roll two six-sided dice, add your stats, and beat the Challenge Score. It's that simple.

Trait Point System

Build your hero from a pool of unique traits. Choose your lineage, path, and specialized skills.

Arcane Mastery

From simple cantrips to world-shaking spells, manage your Mana pool to unleash mystical power.

Our Philosophy

Story Over Stats

We believe the best moments in gaming happen when players describe their actions, not when they calculate modifiers.

Accessibility First

Mythlight is designed for veterans and newcomers alike. If you have two dice and a dream, you're ready to play.
