Equipment

Weapons, armor, and gear define how you survive Mythlight’s world. Equipment provides damage, protection, tags, and tactical options that shape both combat and exploration.

Damage Types

Puncture
Precise strikes; effective against light armor.
Slash
Cutting attacks that cause bleeding effects.
Blunt
Heavy impact; effective against armored targets.
Cleave
Wide, forceful swings that threaten multiple foes.
Blast
Area damage affecting multiple targets.

Brewing & Alchemy

Brewing combines ingredient aspects into deterministic outcomes based on balance, tier, and precision.

Brewing Formula

  • Sum aspect values across ingredients
  • Calculate disparity between highest and others
  • Determine tier by majority ingredient tier
  • DC = 6 + Bits + Disparity + (Tier − 1)
